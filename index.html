Pure Emotion

The Revuelto defines a new paradigm in terms of performance, sportiness and driving pleasure from its unprecedented new architecture; innovative design; maximum efficiency aerodynamics; and a new carbon frame concept.

V12 Evolution

An output of 1015 CV is delivered from the combined power of an entirely new combustion engine together with three electric motors, alongside a double-clutch gearbox that makes its debut on a 12-cylinder Lamborghini for the first time.

Iconic Lines

The signature Y-shape lights and hexagon exhaust ports are the visual manifestation of a revolution in design, keeping the unmistakable Lamborghini DNA while pushing it into the future.

AERODYNAMICS REFINED

Efficiency meets aggression. Every line serves a purpose, channeling air to cool components and generate massive downforce. The active rear wing adjusts automatically to driving modes, optimizing drag and stability at high speeds.

Downforce Efficiency +61%

MONOFUSELAGE

Drawing inspiration from aeronautics, the chassis reduces weight while drastically increasing torsional stiffness. The entire front section is made of Forged Composites, a special material patented by Lamborghini.

Torsional Stiffness +25%

PREMIUM
ARCHITECTURE

The Revuelto features a carbon fiber monocoque structure, delivering exceptional rigidity and lightweight performance. This is the first chassis with a monolithic rocker ring made of carbon fiber.

The specific power of 127 CV/liter is the highest in the history of Lamborghini's 12-cylinder engines, while the maximum torque of 725 Nm corresponds to an enthralling sound.
